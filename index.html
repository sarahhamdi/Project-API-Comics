Outside The Panels

An LGBTQ* & POC Comics Database

We think People of Colour and LGBTQ* makers and characters are important in comics. That’s why we built this tool to help you find them!

POC = People of Colour

LGBTQ* = Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual, Trans, Queer, Questioning, Intersex, 2 Spirit, Asexual, Pansexual

Made with ♥ by Sarah Hamdi, who is not-so-secretly a vampire slayer.

Comic contributions from the Scoobies: Cindy Ma, Heather Mawby, Keira Mawby, May El-Abdallah, and David Missio.
